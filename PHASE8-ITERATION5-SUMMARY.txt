ROOT CAUSE: WHY TOP_P 0.9 FAILS
================================================================================

Problem: Excess diversity breaks multi-step coherence

Mechanism:
1. top_p 0.8 (v2.2): ~5-12 candidate tokens per position
   - Balanced: High-prob mass + controlled diversity
   - Allows multi-step sequencing to work

2. top_p 0.9 (v2.4): ~8-15 candidate tokens per position
   - Excess diversity pulls model away from high-prob continuations
   - Breaks coherence: search→read sequencing fails

3. Interaction with temperature 0.3:
   - Low temp (0.3) + high diversity (top_p 0.9) = conflicting signals
   - Model selects low-coherence tokens instead of continuing sequence
   - Results in TC7, TC8 failures (stops after first tool call)

Evidence:
- TC7 (search→read): Expected 2 calls, got 1 → model stopped after search
- TC8 (list→search→read): Expected 2+ calls, got 1 → model stopped after list
- Single-tool tests (TC1-TC6): All pass (diversity doesn't hurt single calls)
